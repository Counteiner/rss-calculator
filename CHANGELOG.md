## [1.0.0] - 2026-07-01

### Added
- Initial release of Resource Calculator (RCalc) Android app.
- Scan screenshot from gallery, detect 4 resources (Food, Wood, Stone, Gold).
- OCR via ML Kit Text Recognition (on-device, offline).
- Automatic summation of "From Items" and "Total Resources" columns.
- Compact K/M/B number formatting (ported from `resource_ocr.py`).
- Result image rendering with summary panel (ported from `resource_ocr.py`).
- History storage with Room/SQLite database.
- Save result image and share to other apps.
- Target Android 8.0+ (API 26).
